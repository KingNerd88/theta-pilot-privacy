Support

For help, privacy questions, bug reports, or feedback, email kingnerdishere@gmail.com.

Include these details

  • App name and version
  • Windows or Android version, plus device/model where relevant
  • A clear description and steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or error text, with personal information removed

Please do not send passwords, payment-card data, full financial account details, or other unnecessary sensitive information.

App-specific support

When an app has its own support instructions, those instructions are listed on its legal page and in its store listing. Purchase, cancellation, and refund requests may also be subject to the rules of the store through which you purchased the app.

Feature requests

Feedback is welcome. Sending an idea does not create an obligation for KingNerd to implement it or provide compensation, but it helps us decide what to improve.
