Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 July 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how KingNerd ("KingNerd", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information in connection with our Windows and Android applications and this website.

This document sets the baseline for all KingNerd applications. Each application's own legal page and its official store privacy disclosure describe that application's actual data practices in detail. Where an app-specific notice differs from this policy, the app-specific notice applies to that application.

Who we are

KingNerd is an independent software developer based in Romania that designs and publishes applications for Windows and Android. For any privacy question, request, or concern, you can contact us at kingnerdishere@gmail.com. Where required by law, we act as the data controller for the personal information described in this policy.

Scope of this policy

This policy applies to information processed through our applications and this website. It does not apply to third-party products, services, or websites that we do not control, even where they are linked from or integrated with our software. We encourage you to review the privacy notices of any third-party services you use alongside our applications.

Information we collect and why

An application may process only the information needed to provide the features you choose to use. Depending on the application, this may include:

  • Information you provide. Settings, preferences, and records or entries you create within an application, as well as the content of any support message you send us.
  • Purchase and licence status. Confirmation of purchases, subscriptions, or licences, typically provided by the app store or payment provider rather than collected directly by us.
  • Technical and diagnostic data. Limited information such as app version, device or operating-system type, and error or crash diagnostics, where an application uses such reporting to maintain reliability.

The relevant app-specific notice explains, for each application, the exact categories of information, the purpose of processing, where the information is stored, whether it is shared, how long it is retained, and whether a particular feature is optional.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use your information for targeted advertising unless an app-specific notice clearly states otherwise and, where required, you have consented.

Legal bases for processing

Where data-protection law such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation applies, we process personal information only where we have a lawful basis to do so. Depending on the context, this may be: performance of a contract or to provide a feature you request; our legitimate interests in operating, securing, and improving our software; compliance with a legal obligation; or your consent, which you may withdraw at any time.

Storage, sharing, and third parties

Some applications store data locally on your device; others may use a service you select, or a service that is necessary to provide a particular feature. Depending on the application, information may be processed by platform providers (such as Google Play or Microsoft), payment providers, hosting providers, or feature-specific service providers acting on our behalf. The relevant app page and store listing will identify the services that apply.

We do not receive your payment-card details when purchases are handled by an app store or payment platform. Those providers process payment details under their own terms and privacy notices.

We may also disclose information where we are legally required to do so, to protect the rights, safety, or property of KingNerd or others, or in connection with a business transfer, subject to appropriate safeguards.

International data transfers

Because we and some of our service providers may operate in different countries, your information may be processed outside the country in which you live. Where we transfer personal information internationally, we take steps to ensure it receives an appropriate level of protection consistent with applicable law.

Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or the applicable app-specific notice, to comply with our legal obligations, to resolve disputes, and to enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or anonymise it. Where data is stored solely on your device, its retention is generally within your control.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures designed to protect information against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your device, accounts, and credentials secure.

Permissions and sensitive information

Applications request only the permissions relevant to their documented features. You can decline or revoke most permissions through your device settings, although a related feature may then stop working. Please do not enter sensitive information unless it is necessary for a feature and you understand the applicable app-specific notice.

Your rights and choices

Subject to applicable law, you may have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, to request its correction or deletion, to object to or restrict certain processing, to withdraw consent, and to request a copy of certain information in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kingnerdishere@gmail.com. Your specific rights depend on the information involved and the law that applies to you.

Where data is stored only on your device, you can usually manage or delete it directly through the application or your device settings. Individuals in the EEA, the UK, and certain other jurisdictions also have the right to lodge a complaint with their local data-protection authority.

Children's privacy

Our applications are not directed to children unless an app-specific notice expressly states otherwise, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so that we can review and, where appropriate, delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. The effective date above indicates when this policy was last revised. Material app-specific changes will be described in the affected application's notice where required. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please contact us at kingnerdishere@gmail.com.
