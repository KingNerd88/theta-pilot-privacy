Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 July 2026

This policy is the baseline for KingNerd applications. Each app page and its store privacy disclosure identify that app’s actual data practices. Where they differ, the app-specific notice applies.

Who we are

KingNerd develops Windows and Android applications. For privacy questions, contact kingnerdishere@gmail.com.

Information and purposes

An app may process information needed for the features you choose, such as settings, records you enter, support messages, purchase status, or technical diagnostics. The app-specific notice explains the categories, purpose, storage location, sharing, retention, and whether a feature is optional.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use data for targeted advertising unless an app-specific notice clearly says otherwise.

Storage, sharing, and third parties

Some apps may store data on your device; others may use a service you select or a service needed to provide a feature. Data may be processed by platform providers (such as Google Play or Microsoft), payment providers, hosting providers, or feature-specific service providers. The relevant app page and store listing will identify applicable services.

We do not receive payment-card details when purchases are handled by a platform. Platform providers process those details under their own terms and privacy notices.

Permissions and sensitive information

Apps request only permissions relevant to their documented features. You can decline or revoke most permissions through your device settings, although a related feature may stop working. Do not enter sensitive information unless it is necessary for a feature and you understand the applicable app notice.

Your choices and rights

You may contact us to ask about personal information we process, request correction or deletion where applicable, object to or restrict certain processing, or raise a privacy concern. Your rights depend on the information and applicable law. If data is stored only on your device, you can usually manage or delete it through the app or device.

People in the EEA, UK, and other jurisdictions may also have the right to complain to their local data-protection authority.

Children

Our apps are not directed to children unless an app-specific notice states otherwise. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, contact us so we can review the request.

Changes

We may update this policy when our practices or legal requirements change. The effective date above shows the latest revision. Material app-specific changes will be described in the affected app’s notice where required.
