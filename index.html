Legal & Support Center

Welcome to the official Legal & Support Center for KingNerd Windows and Android applications. This site brings together our company-wide policies and the app-specific notices that govern how our software works, how we handle your information, and how to reach us for help.

Each released application has its own dedicated legal page, which should be read together with the company-wide documents listed below. Where an app-specific notice differs from a company-wide document, the app-specific notice applies to that application.

Our approach

KingNerd builds practical, reliable software with clear, honest choices around data, permissions, and support. We believe legal documentation should be readable, accurate, and easy to find — not hidden away or written to confuse.

The exact features and data practices of each application are described on its own page and in its official store listing. We keep these documents up to date as our products evolve.
